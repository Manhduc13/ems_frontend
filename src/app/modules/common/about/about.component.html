About Softdreams

Softdreams is a technology company specializing in providing intelligent software solutions that help automate business management. With over 11 years of experience, the company has successfully developed 12+ products in its ecosystem, including: EasyInvoice, EasyCA, EasyBooks, EasyHRM, EasyPos, EasyDocs, EasyTransport, EasyPIT, EasyTicket, EasyKYC,...

With a customer-centric approach, Softdreams aims to build customer trust through convenient, easy-to-use products and dedicated service, always listening and understanding.

Softdreams' mission is to help "Elevate business management" through simplified information technology solutions. Providing SMEs, individuals and business households with solutions to standardize the entire system and optimize management; at the same time, helping large enterprises and corporations own advanced, seamless management systems of world-class standards.
